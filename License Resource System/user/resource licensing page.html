Resource Licensing System

Resource Licensing System and Self-Paced Learning

Resource Licensing System enables corporate organisations, insurers and schools to provide their employees/members/students with:
  • Free and regular access to a range of health, fitness and wellbeing books
  • Some books are accompanied by a simple self-paced learning module

RESOURCES

Topic Areas

  • General fitness
  • Aerobic fitness
  • Strength and flexibility
  • Stress management and mental health
  • Career satisfaction
  • General health

Format and Reading

The books vary in size from 20 to 40 pages in A4 format. They can be downloaded and printed or read online. Employees answer a short set of questions in the self-paced learning module and confirm they have read the book(s). Its in the interest of employers to have a systematic work health and fitness policyan aspect of which is encouraging staff to read this information on a monthly (or regular) basis.
Click here to view the resources
Once youve expressed interest in the Fitness Frontline resources system youll be able to download and read the books and determine suitability for your employees/members/students.

THE NECESSITY

The health, fitness and wellbeing of the Australian population is in decline. An increasing number of people are in poor metabolic, musculo-skeletal and mental health. This state of affairs affects individuals, the community, the workplace and schools.

Objectives of the Corporate Resources Licensing System

  • Improvement in the health, fitness and wellbeing of staff
  • Enhanced productivity and staff morale
  • A reduction in the number of claims for workers compensation. No longer will employees be able to say, I was never given any information about
  • Lower workers compensation premiums
As an example, failure to provide information and have proof that staff members have read it, could save your organisation (and your workers compensation insurer) up to $500,000. In future editions of the system well add health, fitness and wellbeing assessments.

PROGRAM EXPANSION (Rungs)

The system can be expanded with staff completing a range of assessments in the My Fitness Record book and website and being incentivised to exercise regularly.

For Health Insurers

  1. First rung: Provide members with regular information to get them more involved in looking after their health, fitness and wellbeing.
  2. Second rung: Incentivise members to complete self-paced learning modules.
  3. Third rung: Members complete assessments.
  4. Fourth rung: Incentivise members to exercise regularly.

For Students

PhysEd teachers can introduce the four rungs and have classes join the 20m run class ranking system.

No ticket No start Policy

With respect to some of the resources, corporate organisations are encouraged to adopt a No ticket No start policy as part of:
  • the induction of new employees, and
  • entry into the workers compensation scheme.
For instance, a condition of employment could be that employees have read and completed the Back in Alignment, Safe Lifting and Sit up Straight books and questionnaires.

Musculo-skeletal Health Assessment

A musculo-skeletal health assessment is in the pipeline. Both employers and workers compensation insurers need to know the risk of employing people who are in poor musculo-skeletal health and put procedures in train to reduce the riskand adjust premiums accordingly. The old medical fog up the mirror and tap on the knee with a tack hammer doesnt provide enough information about how fit someone is for your workplace.

HOW THE SYSTEM WORKS FOR CORPORATE ORGANISATIONS

  1. Corporate organisations purchase a yearly license to provide their staff with access to the books and questionnaires, together with the facility for storing the information that the books have been read.
  2. The basic fee structure for corporate organisations is $20 per staff member. In many cases, the investment in the Resources Licensing System will be more than covered by savings in workers compensation arrangements.
  3. If youre interested, please contact us using the Yes, Im interested link below. Well explain how the system works and discuss the financial arrangements. Youll be given access to the system to get the hang of it and decide whether it suits your organisation.
  4. Once your account has been established:
  • Your Work Health and Safety (WHS) management team will have access via LOGIN to the Work Health and Fitness Record system and will know which employees have read a particular book and completed the short self-paced learning module.
  • Your staff will have access via a LOGIN to all the books, audio files and self-paced learning modules via an access code specific to your organisation.
